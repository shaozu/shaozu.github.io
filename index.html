Shaozu CAO | 曹绍祖

I am a PhD graduate of HKUST Aerial Robotics Group, supervised by Prof. Shaojie SHEN.

At HKUST I mainly focused on high-precision and globally consistent state estimation with multiple low-cost sensors. By fusing the information from various low-cost and small-footprint sensors, I aim to design versatile, robust and accurate localization solutions for general spatial-aware applications.

Research

I'm interested in SLAM, computer vision, multi-sensor fusion and state estimation for real-world applications. I also love aerial robots such as quadrotors, helicopters and fixed-wing airplanes.
